About the Studio

Art LaVey Tattoo Studio, a private, family-run studio in Stanley, County Durham, where artistry meets professionalism. Led by Paul LaVey, a multi-award-winning tattoo artist with multi awards from the UK and India, and Faye LaVey, our studio brings over 20 years of combined experience in creating breathtaking tattoos.

Specialising in colour realism, black and grey, and blackwork, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality, custom tattoos in a relaxed, private setting. As a licensed studio for the Halloween franchise and Lucasfilm Ltd, we take pride in offering officially recognised designs for fans of these iconic universes.

At Art LaVey Tattoo Studio, we believe in providing a welcoming, professional experience for every client. Whether you're seeking a highly detailed portrait, bold blackwork, or a cinematic-inspired piece, we can cater to your ideas.

By appointment only.

Artist Profiles

Paul LaVey

Paul LaVey is an award-winning tattoo artist known for his expertise in colour realism and black and grey work. Licensed by Lucasfilm Ltd after working at Star Wars Celebration London with the Ink Fusion Empire, his skill and precision have earned him recognition across the UK with multiple awards, and in India. He's also one of two artists in Europe officially licensed by the official Halloween franchise, the other being his wife.

A BarberDTS Pro Team artist, Paul is also affiliated with Ink Maps Tattoo App, Yayo Familia, and the Ink Fusion Empire. He is proudly sponsored by Tatsoul, Critical Tattoo Supply, Eternal Ink, and Jetblack Supply, solidifying his place among the industry's elite.

Renowned for his artistry and innovation, Paul continues to push creative boundaries, making him a sought-after name in the tattoo world.

Faye LaVey

Faye LaVey is a licensed artist for the official Halloween franchise, specialising in black and grey tattoos, with a passion for portraits and blackwork. Whether creating intricate mandalas or eerie, spooky designs, her work is defined by precision and artistry.

Originally a realistic portrait artist, Faye's love for tattooing began at a young age, shaping her into the skilled artist she is today.
